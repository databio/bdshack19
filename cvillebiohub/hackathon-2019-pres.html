Single-cell technologies

Motivation

  • For an individual organism, there are many cells
  • All with same DNA
  • But many with different characteristics
  • Bulk omics assays present averages
  • Miss important cell-to-cell heterogeneity

Transcriptomics

Early single-cell expression

  • Brady, et al., 1990 profile individual macrophages
  • Eberwine, et al., 1992 profile individual neurons

Original single-cell RNA-seq

  • Tang, et al., 2009 profile individual mouse blastomeres and oocytes

Contemporary methods

  • Hwang, et al., 2018 review single-cell RNA-seq methods
  • Isolation: dilution, micromanipulation, FACS, LCM, microfluidics, CellSearch
  • Library prep: Smart-seq, MARS-seq, CEL-seq, Drop-seq

Epigenomics

  • Smallwood, et al., 2014 introduce scBS-seq
  • Buenrostro, et al., 2015 introduce scATAC-seq
  • Rotem, et al., 2015 introduce scChIP-seq
